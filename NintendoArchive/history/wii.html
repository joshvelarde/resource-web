Wii

The Wii, released on November 19, 2006 (North America), was a revolutionary home console that emphasized motion controls through the Wii Remote, making gaming more accessible to a wide audience. With best-selling titles like Wii Sports, Mario Kart Wii, and Super Smash Bros. Brawl, the console became a cultural phenomenon, attracting casual and hardcore players alike. It also introduced the Virtual Console, allowing players to download classic games from previous Nintendo systems.

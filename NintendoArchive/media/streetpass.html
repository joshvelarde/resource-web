Streetpass Mii Plaza

StreetPass Mii Plaza is a built-in application for the Nintendo 3DS, launched alongside the system on February 26, 2011 (Japan) / March 27, 2011 (North America). It allows players to collect and interact with Mii characters from other 3DS users via StreetPass, a feature that exchanges data when two consoles are in close proximity. The app includes a variety of mini-games, such as Find Mii and Puzzle Swap, with additional paid content introduced in later updates.
